Tenant metering and billing services

Following a thorough consultation within the Property Management sector, Energy Portfolio have developed a metering and billing solution that ensures this crucial function is delivered as a complete solution.

Our metering and billing solution provides the following:
  • A bespoke system for tenant billing and tariff setting
  • Reconciliation of usage and utility charges
  • Data collection via meter reading software
  • Pre-handover inspections
  • Input on legalities and lease reviews
  • Ongoing network management

Water procurement for managing agents

Managing agents and our multi-site clients now have the power to switch their business water retailer. As a business, this allows you to compare business water rates and change to a cheaper tariff.

With the business market water open to competition in England and Scotland, businesses in these parts of the UK can take advantage of the more competitive business water prices available.

Benefits of Energy Portfolio’s water procurement services include:
  • Competitive water prices
  • We ensure smooth transition of transfer of supply
  • Supplier choice based on your business requirements
  • Independent and impartial advice
  • Improved invoicing and commercial arrangements with the new water provider
  • Ongoing support

Choosing the most beneficial supplier for your business

Once we understand your current position, we approach the marketplace to ‘price’ your new contract.

We’ll provide you with a summary of shortlisted suppliers, ranked in order depending on their quotation and customer service levels.

Once you are confident your business has chosen the most beneficial supplier, we will manage the switching process to ensure all your supply is migrated to your new provider.

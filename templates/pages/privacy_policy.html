We collect Personally Identifiable Information only when Users submit form content on Meter Reading and Contact Us sections of this website.

Users may be asked for, as appropriate, name, email address, website address. Users may, however, visit our Websites anonymously. Users can always refuse to supply Personally Identifiable Information, except that it may prevent them from engaging in certain Websites related activities.

We only provide articles and information. We never ask for credit card numbers. We use regular Malware Scanning.

We adopt appropriate data collection, storage and processing practices and security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure or destruction of your personal information, username, password and data stored on our Websites.

Yes. We use a session cookie to keep you logged into the client area during your visit and, optionally, to keep you logged in for future client area visits.

To personalize user experience only.

We do not sell, trade, or rent Users Personally Identifiable Information to others.
